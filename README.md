# CodeAlpha_Phishing-Awareness-Training
A cybersecurity awareness module focused on phishing attacks, social engineering techniques, and best practices to identify and prevent phishing through real-world examples and interactive quizzes.

This project is a Phishing Awareness Training module created to educate users about phishing attacks, social engineering techniques, and safe online practices. It focuses on recognizing phishing emails, fake websites, and understanding how attackers manipulate users.

Topics Covered
What phishing is and why it is dangerous
Common types of phishing attacks
How to identify phishing emails and fake websites
Social engineering tactics used by attackers
Best practices to avoid phishing
Actions to take when phishing is suspected

Social Engineering Techniques
Attackers exploit:
Urgency and fear
Trust and authority
Rewards and curiosity
Familiarity and routine requests

Interactive Quiz
The training includes multiple-choice and true/false questions to test awareness and reinforce learning through real-world phishing scenarios.

Learning Outcome
Users gain the ability to recognize phishing attempts, avoid social engineering traps, and follow secure online behavior.
